Location: Ragnar Anchorage
Date: During the fall

The Cylons managed to pin down a small colonial fleet at Ragnar.

The fleet is currently assembling and preparing it'S escape while hiding in the nebula sorrounding the station.
Ragnar is an old arms and refuelling depot the Cylons managed to board and almost destroy during the first war. It now functions as a warehouse at the edge of the colonies.
After almost 12 hours of just waiting the Cylons decided it is time to engage the fleet in combat, having waited long enough for this day they enter the nebula.
Little do they know what this old station is still capable of...
